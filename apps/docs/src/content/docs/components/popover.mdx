**Popover** reveals an anchored, non-modal panel from its trigger â€” richer than a `Tooltip` (it can hold a heading, body copy, and a close button) and lighter than a `Dialog` (it doesn't trap the page behind a backdrop). A **closed-prop** primitive (ADR-0003) built on Zag.js's `popover` machine, positioned with popper. Like `Tooltip`, it takes the trigger as `triggerLabel` (string) and renders its own button.
